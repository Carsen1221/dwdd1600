Fertilizer

Fertilizer crucial to the plants health by giving them the nutrients that they don't get from the soil. This nutrients helps them grow healthy, strong, and productive. Plants require a variety of nutrients for proper growth, and this fertilizer is a great supplement to enhance the availability of these nutrients in the soil for many different plants. Here are the three main kinds of plant fertilizer:

Nitrogen
Primarily responsible for leafy green growth and vegetative development in plants.
Phosphorus
Crucial for strong root systems, flower and fruit production, and overall plant health.
Potassium
Helps plants withstand environmental stressors like extreme temperatures and drought.
